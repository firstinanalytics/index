First-In Analytics is changing the way 911 calls are dispatched.

In the past, EMS analytics have revolved around historical data, analyzed for trends, and pushed out in a broad attempt to predict the next call. With machine learning, First-In Analytics works to predict the next call based on a multitide of criteria and send the best unit.

The result is a platform that increases response time accuracy, decreases caregiver fatigue, and keeps up with all local trends as they happen.
